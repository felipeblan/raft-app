# Why Raft Exists

Bitcoin education teaches why Bitcoin matters.

But many people still can't answer questions like:

- How much can I actually afford to put into Bitcoin?
- What happens if Bitcoin drops 50%?
- How does this fit with my bills, savings, and other goals?

## Why It Matters

Bitcoin communities often give advice like:

- "Have an emergency fund first."
- "Only invest what you can afford."
- "Dollar‑cost average to manage risk."

But there is no tool that helps someone see:

- Do I actually have an emergency fund?
- How long will it last if things get tight?
- What really happens after I start stacking?
- Can I handle the volatility given my own situation?

The advice exists. The visibility does not.

## What Raft Does

Raft makes tradeoffs visible.

It helps people explore what changes when Bitcoin becomes part of their financial picture, using their own numbers: income, bills, spending, and savings.

It shows what happens under different scenarios without recommending anything, without tracking anything, and without requiring accounts.

No advice. No data collection. Just a clearer view of your situation.

## Privacy

All calculations happen locally on your device. No data is ever sent anywhere. No accounts. No servers. No surveillance.
